The Good, The Bad, And The Ugly

Designing a website isn't easy. There are many factors involved and not every website has it down pack.

The Good

Websites should have a clean, organized, and aesthetically pleasing quality to their overall design.
It is easy to understand navigation appropriate animation, the site purpose is clear to the visitor, navigation is easy to understand, has a good use of color schemes and keeps consistent design elements.

The Bad

While it is hard to say what is bad versus what is ugly, one of the biggest headaches a designer can make is not using a grid system
An inadequate navigational structure with poor typography that makes a website unreadable and random use of colors can make a site fall into this category. Additional things to look out for are like slow site loading speeds and accessibility issues across platforms & devices.

The Ugly

Websites that fall under this category do have not only have to be visually unappealing, but also frustrating.
They often can force the visitor to what may feel like infinite scrolling just to get to secondary information at the bottom of the site like contact info, etc. Over the top animations can also make it "ugly" as well as forcing the visitor to go through "hoops" just to find info.

Examples of Good Websites

Trends are always changing and the advancement of the web and all of its tools and functionalities are making web browsing much more engaging and informative.

Simply Chococate

You immediately know what this website is about about, colors are pleasing and the navigation is clear. It is als interactive in a way that makes sense.

Relish Mama

Uses an enticing image with an overlay of text which is readable and clear as to what the webiste is about, works on all platforms, and clean and the purpose of the site is clear to the visitor.
 

Green Mountain Energy

I actually use this company in conjuction to my utility bill. While not trying to be bias, the site is loads quickly, I always am navigated to the correct forms of information, and it is pleasing to eye.
